Before Google Ads goes live, please audit and fix the lead email flow for `/reduza`.

Current requirement:
Every successful form submission must:

1. Save the lead to Google Sheets.
2. Upload the bill to Google Drive if attached.
3. Send the lead/customer a confirmation email.
4. Send an internal lead alert email to Callum with all submitted details and a direct WhatsApp link for the customer.

Please inspect the current submit route and email service first, then report back exactly:

* what confirmation email is currently sent to the customer;
* what internal email is currently sent to Callum;
* which env vars control sender/from/recipient;
* whether failures in internal email are fatal or non-fatal;
* whether the phone field is labelled and stored as WhatsApp.

Required frontend/form change:
The phone field should be labelled as WhatsApp, not just generic telephone.

Use Portuguese label:

`WhatsApp`

or:

`Telefone / WhatsApp`

The submitted field may still be called `phone` internally, but the label and email/sheet copy should make clear this is the customer’s WhatsApp number.

Required internal notification email:
Send immediately to:

`callum@otimaenergia.com`

Use env var if already available:

`OTIMA_INTERNAL_LEAD_EMAIL`

Default fallback:

`callum@otimaenergia.com`

Subject format:

`Novo lead Ótima — {{company}} — {{averageEnergyBill}} — {{state}}`

If company is missing, use name.

Email body must include:

* Lead ID
* Submitted at
* Name
* Company
* Email
* WhatsApp / phone
* City
* State
* Average energy bill
* Property type
* Bill uploaded? yes/no
* Bill file URL if present
* Message
* Lead status
* UTM source
* UTM medium
* UTM campaign
* UTM term
* UTM content
* GCLID
* GBRAID
* WBRAID
* Landing page URL
* Referrer
* User agent
* IP address

Also include a prominent WhatsApp action link.

Build the WhatsApp link from the submitted phone number:

* strip all non-digits;
* if the number does not start with Brazil country code `55`, prefix `55`;
* generate:

`https://wa.me/{{normalizedPhone}}`

Also include a prefilled WhatsApp message link:

`https://wa.me/{{normalizedPhone}}?text={{encodedMessage}}`

Suggested prefilled message in Portuguese:

`Olá {{name}}, aqui é o Renan da Ótima Energia. Recebemos sua solicitação de análise gratuita para reduzir a conta de energia da {{company}}. Posso te chamar por aqui para confirmar alguns dados?`

The internal email should show:

`Abrir WhatsApp do lead`

as a clickable button/link.

Also include:

`Enviar para Renan`

as a mailto link if possible, prefilled with all lead details, OR include a clean copy/paste block that Callum can forward to Renan.

Required customer confirmation email:
Send to the customer email immediately after successful submission.

From should be the configured Ótima sender, preferably:

`marketing@otimaenergia.com`

If sender is env controlled, use:

`OTIMA_EMAIL_FROM`

Subject:

`Recebemos sua solicitação — Ótima Energia`

Portuguese body:

```
Olá {{name}},

Recebemos sua solicitação para a análise gratuita da sua conta de energia.

Nossa equipe irá revisar as informações enviadas e um Especialista em Energia da Ótima entrará em contato com sua comparação personalizada.

Se você já enviou sua conta de energia, não precisa fazer mais nada neste momento.

Se ainda não enviou, você pode responder este email com a conta em anexo para agilizar a análise.

Atenciosamente,
Equipe Ótima Energia
```

Important:

* Do not expose internal notes, GCLID, UTMs, or tracking data in the customer email.
* Customer confirmation email failure should be logged.
* Internal Callum email is operationally important. If Google Sheets save succeeds but internal email fails, submission can still return success, but log the full error loudly with lead ID.
* If Google Sheets save fails, continue current hard-failure behaviour unless the existing architecture intentionally differs.
* If bill upload was attempted and fails, preserve the current hard-failure behaviour.
