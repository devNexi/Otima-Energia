Website Design Brief: Ótima Energia
To: Replit Design Team
Project: Ótima Energia Corporate Website
Timeline: 2–3 weeks for design + development
Reference: [Business Plan PDF]

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
We are building Ótima Energia, a high-performance, tech-augmented energy brokerage launching in Brazil’s newly deregulated electricity market. We are not a traditional broker—we are a data-driven, transparency-first company that uses AI and automation to deliver the best prices and service to business clients.

The website must reflect:

Professionalism & Trust (financial/energy sector)

Modern Tech-Forward vibe (AI, automation, data intelligence)

Brazilian market context (local credibility, Portuguese language)

Target Audience:

Brazilian business owners & facility managers (SMEs → large industrials)

Energy procurement officers

Potential commercial partners & investors

2. BRAND IDENTITY & TONE
Name: Ótima Energia (“Excellent Energy” in Portuguese)

Tagline: “Transparência, tecnologia e o melhor preço.” (Transparency, technology, and the best price.)

Tone: Confident, clear, innovative, trustworthy, slightly disruptive.

Key Message: We use technology to remove opacity from energy buying, guaranteeing you the best market price with minimal effort.

Visual Direction:

Clean, professional, corporate but not stale

Use of blue (trust, energy), green (sustainability), and tech accents (data visualizations, sleek UI elements)

High-quality imagery: modern Brazilian cityscapes, businesses, clean energy visuals, data dashboards (conceptual)

3. WEBSITE STRUCTURE (PAGES)
Homepage
Hero section: Strong headline + subheadline in Portuguese

Value prop: “How we guarantee you the best price” (3–4 icons/points: AI-driven comparison, supplier network, transparency, no hassle)

Simple lead form: “Get your free quote now” (UC code/upload bill)

Trust signals: ANEEL/CCEE badges, “Data-secure”

Brief intro to team/leadership (Callum & Renan photos + titles)

How It Works (Como Funciona)
Step-by-step visual process:

You share your bill/UC code

Our AI scans the entire market

We present the best 3 options (clear comparison table)

You choose, we handle the switch

Emphasize no cost to client (we are paid by suppliers)

For Businesses (Para Empresas)
Segmented: SMEs vs. Large Industrials

Case studies/testimonials (placeholder for now)

Benefits: cost reduction, time saved, contract clarity

Transparency & Technology (Transparência & Tecnologia)
Explain our tech edge: AI, automation, supplier APIs

Our pricing transparency promise: no hidden margins

Data security & compliance

About Us (Quem Somos)
Mission: To modernize Brazil’s energy market

Team bios: Callum (UK tech/strategy), Renan (Brazil ops/network)

Company backstory: Part of ACR Group, building future market infrastructure

Contact (Contato)
Contact form

Email: contato@otimaenergia.com.br

WhatsApp business link (critical for Brazil)

Office location (São Paulo/remote)

4. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
Core Features:
Lead Capture Form: Simple form for UC code/consumption details + file upload for energy bill

WhatsApp Integration: Prominent WhatsApp Business button (floating)

Bilingual Support: Portuguese primary, English toggle (for investors/partners)

Mobile-First: Must work perfectly on mobile (high mobile usage in Brazil)

Fast Loading: Optimized for Brazilian internet speeds

SEO-Ready: Structure for Brazilian search terms (“trocar fornecedor de energia empresarial,” “melhor preço energia mercado livre”)

Nice-to-Have (Phase 1):
Live chat (Drift/Crisp)

Interactive savings calculator

Blog section (for SEO/content marketing)

5. TECHNICAL SPECS
Framework: Next.js or similar (fast, SEO-friendly)

Hosting: Vercel or AWS São Paulo region

CMS: Simple (Sanity.io or WordPress headless) for easy updates

Domain: otimaenergia.com.br

Security: SSL, GDPR/LGPD compliant forms
