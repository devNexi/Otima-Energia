🚨 Critical Architectural Flaws in Your Approach

Your KPI Logic is Fundamentally Broken. Your "Quote Win Rate" formula (deals won) / (RFQs sent) is wrong and will produce useless data. An RFQ can have 5 suppliers. You're Ignoring the Core Data Relationship. The critical link for KPIs is between a specific supplier's quote and the final awarded deal. You need to ensure the dealQuotes table has a supplier_id foreign key (it likely does). If not, this is your first task, not building logs.
