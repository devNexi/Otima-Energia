PART 2 — PRICE BAND PHILOSOPHY (THIS SOLVES YOUR COMMISSION FEAR)

This is crucial. Read carefully.

The Core Rule

ECOS never optimises to the lowest possible price.
It optimises to the optimal price band.

That distinction saves your business.

1. What a Price Band Is

A price band represents:

Competitive market pricing

Sustainable supplier economics

Stable broker commissions

Reduced churn

It is not the market floor.

2. How Price Bands Are Defined

For each segment, define:

Lower Bound
Price below which:

Supplier quality drops

Contract risk increases

Commission becomes unsustainable

Upper Bound
Price above which:

Client is objectively overpaying

Renegotiation is justified

Anything inside the band = acceptable.

3. How ECOS Uses the Band
Scenario A — Client inside band

Status: 🟢

Recommendation: Hold

Messaging:

“Your contract is competitive given current market conditions.”

➡️ No margin erosion
➡️ No unnecessary renegotiation

Scenario B — Client slightly above band

Status: 🟡

Recommendation: Monitor

Messaging:

“We are monitoring the market for a better entry point.”

➡️ Buys time
➡️ Manages expectations

Scenario C — Client materially above band

Status: 🔴

Recommendation: Renegotiate

Messaging:

“Current pricing is no longer aligned with the market.”

➡️ Action is justified
➡️ Supplier conversation is clean

4. Why This Protects Your Commission

Because:

You are not promising “cheapest”

You are promising “correct”

You act only when value is real

You don’t train clients to chase noise

This aligns:

you

client

supplier
