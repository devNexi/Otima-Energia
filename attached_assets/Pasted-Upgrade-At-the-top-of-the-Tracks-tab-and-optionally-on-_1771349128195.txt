5) Track status transitions: ensure they’re right for GDL

You said transitions are validated (great). But ensure the GDL statuses match the Prime flow and don’t force irrelevant states.

Minimum recommended GDL states:

GDL_NEW

GDL_ELIGIBILITY_PASSED

GDL_DOCS_COMPLETE

GDL_SUBMITTED_TO_PRIME

GDL_PRIME_APPROVED / GDL_PRIME_REJECTED

GDL_PROPOSAL_SENT

GDL_CLIENT_ACCEPTED

GDL_CONTRACT_SIGNED

GDL_CLOSED_WON / GDL_CLOSED_LOST

Make sure:

GDL_SUBMITTED_TO_PRIME is blocked unless docs complete + eligibility passed.

GDL_PRIME_APPROVED can’t happen unless submitted.

GDL_CLIENT_ACCEPTED can’t happen unless proposal sent.

GDL_CLOSED_WON can’t happen unless contract signed (unless you explicitly allow manual override).

6) Do NOT auto-move the Deal stage (but DO evaluate closure)

Your earlier system had Deal stage machine. Tracks must not hijack it.

Best practice:

Tracks can close won/lost independently.

Deal closes when:

any Track = CLOSED_WON → prompt Renan: “Close deal as Won?” (one-click confirm)

all Tracks = CLOSED_LOST → prompt Renan: “Close deal as Lost?”

Otherwise leave Deal stage untouched.

This keeps the Otima RFQ flow intact.
