PRC PDFs vary by supplier and layout.

Parsing strategy (must follow this order)

Try PDF text extraction (fast)

Extraction heuristics

Must detect:

submarket headings (SE/CO, Sul, Norte/NE etc.)

product sections:

Convencional

Incentivada variants i0/i50/i100

term buckets:

“Anual / Trianual / Quinquenal”

or year ranges (convert to months if possible)

price tokens (R$/MWh)

Confidence scoring

For each row:

High confidence if product+term+submarket+price found cleanly

Medium if one field inferred

Low if ambiguous or missing submarket/product/term
Low-confidence rows must appear in the review queue.

Outlier detection

Flag row if price deviates > X% from median of same grouping in the month.
