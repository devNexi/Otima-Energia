Yes — businesses often send documents that are “bill-adjacent” but still RFQ-useful:

NF3e / Nota Fiscal de Energia Elétrica (invoice-style energy doc)

Fatura / Conta de Energia (Distribuidora) standard layouts

Demonstrativo de Faturamento / Demonstrativo de Consumo (summary pages)

Histórico de Consumo / Histórico de Medição (like your case)

Demanda / Fatura de Demanda addendums (especially Grupo A)

Boleto-only pages (payment slip separated from technical pages)

CUSD / Uso do Sistema de Distribuição style breakdown pages (TUSD-heavy)

Fatura de Comercializadora (free-market invoices that include supply + network components)

Relatórios de medição / memória de cálculo (not a bill, but contains demand/consumption values)

Extratos CCEE / Liquidação (less common for your clients directly, but can appear)

Your “one parser to rule them all” approach is right: treat them all as RFQ data sources.
