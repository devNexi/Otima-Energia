Animation Brief: "Market Layer Reveal" - Condensed 8-Second Version
Project: Ótima Energia Homepage Hero Animation
Concept: MARKET LAYER REVEAL (Light Theme)
Duration: 6-8 seconds total (seamless loop)
Goal: Create a minimal, striking background video that communicates transparency, market intelligence, and savings.

VISUAL SEQUENCE (6-8 Second Loop)
0-1s: OPAQUE MARKET
• Clean light gradient background (#f8fafc to #ffffff)
• "Fog layer" of muted, tangled data lines in soft grey (represents market opacity)

1-3s: THE REVEAL
• Sharp cyan line (#00f5d4) sweeps left-to-right
• Fog dissolves cleanly where line passes (vapor/signal wave effect)
• Reveals organized data landscape beneath: minimalist Brazil map outline with glowing price bars

3-5s: DATA LANDSCAPE
• Clean grid of vertical bars in cyan-blue gradient (varying heights = price variations)
• Bars pulse gently with data energy
• Camera subtly zooms toward a business location

5-7s: SAVINGS MOMENT
• One shorter bar highlights with green pulse (#00ff9d)
• Portion of bar elegantly fades/dissolves (representing savings)
• Subtle "+XX%" indicator appears briefly

7-8s: RESET
• Smooth zoom back to original position
• Fog layer gently reforms
• Seamless loop restart

CRITICAL SPECIFICATIONS
Brand Colors:
• Primary Intelligence: Electric Cyan (#00f5d4)
• Savings Highlight: Energy Green (#00ff9d)
• Data/Maps: Cool greys (#e2e8f0 range)
• Background: Light (#f8fafc to #ffffff)

Style Guidelines:
• Minimal & Elegant - Every movement has purpose
• Light theme throughout - Contrast via data visuals, not dark backgrounds
• Smooth, intelligent motion - No jerkiness, feels premium
• Clean typography-ready - Animation supports text overlay without distraction

Technical Requirements:
• Duration: 6-8 seconds total loop
• Format: MP4 + WebM (optimized for web)
• Size: Under 2MB
• Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
• Performance: 60fps if possible, seamless loop
• Overlay-ready: Ensure text remains readable

INSPIRATION & MOOD
Think: "Apple data reveal" meets "light-themed fintech dashboard"
• Clean, corporate, but innovative
• Sophisticated data visualization
• Intelligent movement that feels like a precision tool
• The moment of clarity when confusion disappears

Final Feeling: "This company sees what others can't. They'll find my savings in the noise."

Deliverable: One 6-8 second looping video file (MP4+WebM) that works as a homepage background with text overlay.
